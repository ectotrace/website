Philosophy

Honoring the outer layer.

"Ecto"—the outer boundary. "Trace"—the mapping of form. Our practice is rooted in preserving the natural edges of raw timber and integrating living botanical structures into grid-based spatial designs.

We operate at the intersection of architectural precision and organic irregularity, creating grounded environments.
